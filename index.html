Bumblegum is nummie-nummie and I know you feel the same. But have you every wondered how you can help your local hipster feel the same? Follow this questionable all-natural recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup beeswax
  • 3 tbsp corn syrup
  • 5 drops natural peppermint oil
  • 5 drops pandan extract
  • 1 cup natural stevia

Will make 12 pieces!

Directions:

  • Call your mother and tell her you love her.
  • Don't skip that first step! Treat your mother like the queen she is!
  • Balance glass bowl on top of saucepan of water and heat on medium-high. Melt beeswax in bowl.
  • Stir in corn syrup, peppermint for flavor, panda for color and stevia for sweetness.
  • Pour into candy molds and chill.
  • Once set, remove from molds store in airtight container.
